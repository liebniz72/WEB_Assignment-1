My Favorite Foods

Beef Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful mixed rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent.
It is typically made with a combination of long-grain Basmati rice and spiced meat,
along with a variety of aromatic spices. While there are many variations of biryani,
one popular option is beef biryani, which is made with tender chunks of beef
that are marinated in a blend of spices and then cooked with the rice.

  • Chicken Roast

    • Bengali cuisine is known for its bold and spicy flavors, and one popular dish that exemplifies this is Chicken Roast. This dish is made by marinating chicken in a blend of aromatic spices and then roasting it in the oven or on a stove top until it is tender and juicy. The result is a flavorful twist on a classic dish that is sure to please even the most discerning palate.

  • Yogurt Dessert

    • Doi is a traditional yogurt-based dessert from the Indian subcontinent, and in Bengali cuisine, it is a beloved sweet treat that is enjoyed by people of all ages. This creamy and tangy dessert is made by fermenting milk with a culture of live bacteria, which converts the lactose in the milk into lactic acid, giving it a tangy flavor and thick consistency.
